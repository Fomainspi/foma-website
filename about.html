About FOMA

FOMA — Foundation of Mastering Automation — is more than a learning platform. It is a movement dedicated to shaping the next generation of DevOps engineers, cloud professionals, and automation experts.

In a world driven by speed, scalability, and continuous innovation, mastering automation is no longer optional — it is a fundamental requirement. FOMA exists to equip individuals with the skills, mindset, and practical experience needed to thrive in modern technology environments.

Our Vision

To become a global reference in DevOps education by delivering practical, real-world knowledge that transforms learners into professionals capable of building, automating, and scaling reliable systems.

Our Mission

FOMA is committed to simplifying complex technologies and making them accessible without compromising depth or quality.

  • Deliver hands-on, real-world DevOps training
  • Bridge the gap between theory and practical implementation
  • Promote automation as a core engineering principle
  • Empower individuals to build scalable and resilient systems

What We Stand For

FOMA is built on discipline, consistency, and execution. We believe that true mastery comes from doing — not just learning.

  • Learning by building real systems
  • Automation over repetition
  • Efficiency over complexity
  • Continuous improvement

What You Will Gain

Through FOMA, you will not only learn tools — you will develop the ability to think, design, and operate like a DevOps engineer.

  • Mastery of tools such as Docker, Kubernetes, Terraform, and CI/CD pipelines
  • Understanding of cloud infrastructure and deployment strategies
  • Ability to design scalable and automated systems
  • Confidence to handle real-world engineering challenges

Why FOMA

Because the future belongs to those who can automate, scale, and deliver — consistently.

FOMA is where that journey begins.
